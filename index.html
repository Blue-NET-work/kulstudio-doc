Documentation V 1.0.0

Get your own easily customizable countdown script without any further JS knowledge! Count Everest is quickly integrated into your own markup. It’s extendable with callback functions, provides many options (e.g.: left-hand zeros), and comes with an easy-to-follow documentation. Localize it within seconds. Use your own font. And enjoy it even in Internet Explorer. We can’t wait to see what you create with it.

Quick installation

Step 1: Choose and edit one of the index.html files

Choose the index.html that suits you best and open it to set your target date for the countdown at the end of the file.

Step 2: Upload files

Upload all files and folders via FTP to your webserver.

Step 3: Test it in your browser

Open the index.html that you have chosen in your favourite browser. Done!

Options

Change color theme

If you want to change the default color to the supplied.
Step 1: Find this code in the index.html

Change the file name green.css on your chosen.


		<!-- Theme color -->
  		<link href="assets/css/themes/green.css" rel="stylesheet" id="style-color">
Step 2: Save and upload files

Save and upload files via FTP to your webserver.

Step 3: Test it in your browser

Open the index.html that you have chosen in your favourite browser. Done!
